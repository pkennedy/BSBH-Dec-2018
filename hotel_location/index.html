Bohol South Beach Hotel Location

The hotel is located on Danao Beach, on the south coast of the island of Panglao. It is 16km from the city of Tagbilaran (population 110,000), approximately a 25 minute drive by Bohol South Beach Hotel shuttle van from Tagbilaran Airport on the main island of Bohol.
