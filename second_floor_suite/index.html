Second Floor Deluxe Suite

Second floor deluxe suites provide beautiful ocean views from en elevated vantage point. Each room has a floating bed, with remote control under-bed soft illumination. Standard occupancy for these rooms is four adults. Four more adults or children can also stay in the suite on air mattresses for a small extra fee.

Bohol South Beach Hotel, Danao Beach, Panglao, Bohol, Philippines.
