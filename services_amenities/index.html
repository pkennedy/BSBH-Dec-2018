Services & Amenities

Bohol South Beach has a number of cool amenities, including our Infinity Pool, Rooftop Deck, Dining area (Bar & Cafe) and Poolside Tiki Bar. We have three bars; one Pool-side, one in the Dining area and one on the Rooftop Deck. Our wonderful restaurant meals are also available via Room service. We have a number of bicycles available for guest use for free, and a motorcycle which can be rented for a small sum daily.

Bohol South Beach Hotel, Danao Beach, Panglao, Bohol, Philippines.
